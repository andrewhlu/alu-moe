ScaVision!

A new take on the scavenger hunt!

Race against your opponents to solve riddles and find items faster than them!

Each round, you will get a riddle. The answer to every riddle is an item that's part of your daily lives. When you figure out the answer to the riddle, search your surroundings for the item, and take a picture of it to win!

This app uses your device's camera. Because this app may require moving around, I recommend using a phone or tablet to play this game.

This game utilizes Google Cloud's Vision API to detect items in your pictures. As part of this game, your pictures will be uploaded to Google Cloud.
